Welcome to our Hangman game

  1. each player who guesses the word starts with 230 points and 120 seconds. For a wrong letter he loses 10 points, and every 10 seconds another 15 points.
  2. first choose whether you want to play against another player or the computer (which randomly gives you a word from its database of English words)
  3. when you have chosen the desired option, press the "Start Game" button
  4. if you have chosen the option to play against another player, when a new window opens, one of you enters the word that is guessed so that the other does not see it
  5. then by pressing the "Player 2 start the game" button, the game starts where the player who guesses the word enters one of the letters via the keyboard
  6. if he guessed the letter, it will appear in the place (or places) where it is in the given word
  7. in case he missed, that letter will be printed as wrong, and at the same time a body part will appear on the gallows
  8. the player can make 6 mistakes and then the game ends
  9. at the end, you get a corresponding message and the possibility to restart the game
